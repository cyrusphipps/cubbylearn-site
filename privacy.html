Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Overview

CubbyLearn Animal Explorer (“the App”) is designed to provide a simple, safe, and enjoyable learning experience for young children.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the App works.

The App does not collect, store, or share personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Specifically:

  • No names, emails, or contact information are collected
  • No location data is collected
  • No device identifiers are collected
  • No usage tracking or analytics are performed
  • No voice recordings are stored or transmitted

All interactions within the App occur locally on the device.

3. Data Storage and Processing

  • All App functionality operates entirely on-device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is stored outside of the user’s device

If the App uses voice recognition features, processing occurs using the device’s built-in capabilities and is not stored or transmitted by CubbyLearn.

4. Children’s Privacy

CubbyLearn Animal Explorer is designed for young children.

We comply with applicable children’s privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children or require parental consent for data collection.

5. Advertising and Third Parties

  • The App contains no advertisements
  • The App does not use third-party analytics tools
  • The App does not share data with third parties

6. Internet Connectivity

The App is designed to function without an internet connection.

No data is sent or received by CubbyLearn during normal use of the App.

7. Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, the risk of data exposure is minimized.

We design the App to operate locally and securely on the device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
